While I’ve never seen Minecraft use anywhere near even a Gigabyte of RAM, I’ve configured my system to have access to all 24 Gigabytes of RAM my laptop has.

  1. Open the launcher menu (aka Hamburger Menu).
  2. Switch to the “Launcher options” tab.
  3. Select the Minecraft configuration you want to configure.
  4. Look for an option 
    -Xmx
    . Change that to the amount of RAM you want Minecraft to have.
